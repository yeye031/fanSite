About

Karla Camila Cabello Estrabao is a Cuban-American singer and songwriter. She became famous as a member of the group 'Fifth Harmony'. 'Fifth Harmony' was a group formed during the second season of The X Factor in 2012. Camila Cabello became a solo artist in 2016 due to problems in the group.
